# Google Play Store Listing — MindShift

## 1. App Title (30 chars max)

MindShift — ADHD Focus

## 2. Short Description (80 chars max)

Calm task management and focus sessions built for how ADHD brains actually work.

## 3. Full Description

Your brain works differently. Most productivity apps ignore that, or worse, punish you for it. Missed a day? Red warnings. Forgot a task? Guilt trip. Broke a streak? Start over. None of that belongs in your toolkit.

MindShift is a productivity app designed from the ground up for ADHD. No shame mechanics. No urgency tricks. No overwhelm. Just a calm space that adapts to your energy, your patterns, and your pace.

TASK MANAGEMENT THAT RESPECTS YOUR LIMITS
- Three pools: NOW (max 3 tasks), NEXT (queued up), and SOMEDAY (no pressure)
- Drag to reorder. Voice input to capture thoughts fast.
- Recurring tasks (daily/weekly) with gentle due date reminders
- Task types: tasks, ideas, meetings, reminders across 5 categories
- Missed a deadline? Warm amber nudge, never a red alarm

FOCUS SESSIONS GROUNDED IN NEUROSCIENCE
- Sessions move through struggle, release, and flow phases naturally
- Sound presets: brown noise, pink noise, nature, lofi, gamma binaural
- Phase-adaptive volume that adjusts as you settle into focus
- Surprise timer mode for time-blind sessions (no digits, no arc, just vibes)
- Breathwork ritual to ease into sessions
- Focus rooms: co-work anonymously with others in real time
- Ambient orbit: see how many people are focusing right now

ENERGY-AWARE, NOT GUILT-AWARE
- Rate your energy 1-5 before and after sessions
- Low energy? The app simplifies itself: fewer tasks, calmer UI
- Burnout detection watches for patterns and suggests recovery, not hustle
- Invisible streaks: shown only when you are on a roll, hidden when you take a break
- Shutdown ritual helps you close the day without loose threads
- Weekly planning and monthly reflection rituals keep you grounded

MOCHI — YOUR AI COMPANION
- Mochi is a warm mascot that knows your focus style and energy patterns
- Personalized encouragement based on your psychotype (achiever, explorer, connector, or planner)
- Mochi is not a coach, not a therapist. Just a companion who gets it.
- Age-appropriate tone: gen z, millennial, gen x, or neutral

BUILT FOR YOUR BRAIN
- ADHD-safe color palette: teal, indigo, gold. No red anywhere.
- WCAG AA accessible with full keyboard navigation and screen reader support
- Reduced motion mode for sensory sensitivity
- Works offline as a PWA. Your data stays on your device until you sign in.
- English and Russian language support

PRIVACY FIRST
- No ads. No tracking pixels. No selling your data.
- GDPR compliant with full data export and account deletion
- Guest mode works without any account at all
- Focus rooms are fully anonymous (no names, no profiles shared)

MindShift does not promise to fix your ADHD. It gives you a space that works with your brain instead of against it. Show up when you can. Rest when you need to. That counts.

## 6. Content Rating Justification

MindShift qualifies for an "Everyone" (IARC: 3+) rating.

- **No violence or graphic content.** The app contains no violent imagery, weapons, or conflict.
- **No gambling or simulated gambling.** XP and streaks are non-competitive and have no monetary value.
- **No sexual content.** All imagery and copy is appropriate for all ages.
- **No user-to-user communication with real identity.** Focus rooms use anonymous presence only. Users see a count and phase indicators (e.g., "3 people focusing"). No chat, no usernames, no profile pictures, no way to identify or contact another user.
- **No user-generated content sharing.** There are no feeds, comments, or public posts.
- **No location tracking.** The app does not request or use location data.
- **No ads or third-party ad SDKs.**
- **AI-generated content is limited to short motivational messages** from the Mochi mascot. Messages are pre-filtered by system prompt to exclude medical advice, medication references, and diagnostic language. Content is warm and encouraging only.
