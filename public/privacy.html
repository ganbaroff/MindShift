Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · A Russian translation is available in-app.

Who We Are

MindShift is an ADHD-aware productivity app developed by Yusif Ganbarov. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at ganbarov.y@gmail.com.

What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work for you:

  • Email address — used for authentication (magic link or Google OAuth sign-in).
  • Focus session data — duration, phase reached (struggle / release / flow), and energy levels before and after sessions.
  • Task data — titles, due dates, difficulty, repeat settings, notes, and task type.
  • Onboarding preferences — app mode, psychotype, ADHD signals (time blindness, emotional reactivity).
  • Basic device information — collected by Plausible Analytics (cookieless, no personally identifiable information). Used solely to understand aggregate usage patterns.

Microphone Access

MindShift offers optional voice input for creating tasks. When you use this feature:

  • Audio is processed in real-time by your browser's built-in SpeechRecognition API.
  • We do not record, store, or transmit your audio data.
  • The resulting text transcript is sent to our AI service for task classification, then discarded.
  • You can use MindShift without ever enabling microphone access.

How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize your experience — AI-powered mascot messages (Mochi) are generated using your psychotype, behavior profile, and session context via the Google Gemini API.
  • Generate insights — weekly summaries and task decomposition suggestions are created from your session and task data.
  • Detect focus patterns — session history is analyzed locally to suggest psychotype re-derivation when your habits evolve.
  • Improve the app — aggregate, anonymized analytics help us understand what features are used.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

MindShift relies on the following services to function:

  • Supabase (database, authentication) — data hosted in the EU region. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API (AI features) — used for generating Mochi messages, task decomposition, and weekly insights. Your data is not used by Google for model training. Gemini API Terms.
  • Plausible Analytics — cookieless, GDPR-compliant web analytics. No personal data is collected. Plausible Data Policy.
  • Sentry (error tracking) — captures technical errors to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included in error reports. Sentry Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel (hosting) — serves the application. Vercel Privacy Policy.

Telegram Integration

If you choose to link your Telegram account:

  • Your Telegram user ID is stored to enable communication between you and MindShift's Telegram bot.
  • Messages you send to the bot are processed by AI for task creation.
  • Message content is not stored beyond creating the resulting task.
  • You can unlink Telegram at any time from the app settings.

Cookies

MindShift uses only essential cookies required for Supabase authentication sessions. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party marketing cookies. A cookie consent banner is displayed on your first visit.

Data Retention

Your data is stored for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers. Completed tasks older than 30 days are automatically pruned from local storage.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — export all your data in JSON format using the in-app data export feature.
  • Right to deletion — permanently delete your account and all associated data through the in-app account deletion feature, or by contacting us.
  • Right to data portability — your exported data is provided in a standard, machine-readable JSON format.
  • Right to rectification — you can update your profile, preferences, and task data at any time within the app.
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can disable optional features (AI, microphone, Telegram) at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at ganbarov.y@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

MindShift is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Your continued use of MindShift after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
